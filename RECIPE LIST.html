Angela's recipe for the Best cinnamon rolls

Ingrediants

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cupwarm milk
  • 9/4 tespoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar ,eggs.
  2. melt the butter and add the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with the plastic wrap. leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar ,eggs.
